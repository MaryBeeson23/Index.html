Why are Accordions so COOL?!

Between all the different styles of accordions, and different genres of music they influence, accordions are objectively the coolest instrument. It was invented by Christian Friedrich (who also invented the Harmonica) in 1822. Its been played by people like John Lennon and Elvis Presley.

What are the different styles of accordions?

There are many different styles of accordions, not limited to:

  1. Piano Accordion
  2. Button Accordion
  3. The Squeezebox
  4. The Concertina
  5. The Bandoneon

The Accordion plays a centric role in some genres, such as:

  • Folk
  • Cajun
  • Zydeco
  • Tex-mex
  • Cumbia
  • Argentinian Tango
  • and even some Pop music
